# Privacy Policy for QuickChess Chrome Extension

**Effective Date:** August 24, 2025

## Data Collection and Usage

QuickChess is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what you need to know about how the extension handles your data:

### Information We DO NOT Collect
- **No Personal Information:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, or any other identifying information.
- **No Browsing Data:** We do not track your browsing history, website visits, or online activities.
- **No Analytics:** We do not use any analytics services or tracking tools.
- **No User Accounts:** The extension does not require registration or user accounts.

### Information We Store Locally
The extension stores the following data **locally on your device only**:
- **Game State:** Current chess board position, move history, and game progress
- **Settings:** Your selected difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard)
- **Preferences:** UI settings and configuration options

This data is stored using Chrome's local storage API and **never leaves your device**.

### External Services
- **Google Fonts:** The extension loads the "Playwrite CA" font from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) for visual enhancement. This is a standard web font service and does not collect personal information.

### Data Sharing
- **No Data Sharing:** We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties.
- **No Server Communication:** The extension operates entirely offline after initial font loading.

### Data Security
- All game data remains on your local device
- No data is transmitted over the internet
- No external servers or databases are used

### Children's Privacy
This extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

### Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

### Contact Information
If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please create an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/KshitijKoranne/quick-chess-chrome-extension).

## Summary
QuickChess is a privacy-focused chess extension that stores game data locally on your device and does not collect or transmit any personal information.
